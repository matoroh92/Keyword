IPM Photo Library

Welcome to our Photo Gallery, which includes nearly 1000 images of insect and mites pests, diseases, and abiotic disorders found on the many ornamental plants grown in Connecticut’s greenhouses and outdoor production yards. Many of the photos are by Greenhouse IPM Extension Educator Emeritus Leanne Pundt and other colleagues as part of her Greenhouse IPM and extension activities at UConn Extension.
